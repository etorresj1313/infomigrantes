Our Mission

 

In this social project we want to contribute to a problem that often occurs in our country. This problem is about the population of immigrants who have

recently arrived. Many people who are immigrants often have problems as much as they do in the process of integration and documentation.

Also as a society we are not empathic with them. We want to change this and with what we do we have the security that we can contribute with a grain

of sand to this cause. We want to thank the foreign department that contributed to this project.
